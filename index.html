Vehicle Security & Trip Protection

Comprehensive safety for both car owners and renters

Vehicle Tracking

SwiftHire enhances safety through our partnership with TrackNTrace, allowing car owners to install reliable tracking devices at discounted rates. Soon, users will also be able to monitor their vehicles directly within the app through integrated tracking APIs, ensuring real-time oversight and peace of mind.

Trip Insurance

Every trip on SwiftHire is secured through our partnerships with trusted insurance providers. This ensures that both the car owner and the renter are protected against unexpected incidents throughout the rental period, delivering a safer, more confident experience for all users.

Secure Verification

To keep the platform safe, SwiftHire uses a strict verification process for all vehicle owners before their cars can be listed. Payments are also securely handled—funds are only released to the owner once the renter confirms the car's condition by selecting “Start Trip” after inspection, preventing fraud and promoting trust across the platform

Garage Network

SwiftHire is building a network of verified local garages to provide quick support whenever mechanical issues arise. Renters will easily locate and contact the nearest approved garage directly from the app, ensuring fast assistance and minimal disruption during a trip.
